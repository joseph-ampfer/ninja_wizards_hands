## System Requirements

### Minimum Requirements
- **OS:** Windows 10 or later
- **Processor:** Intel Core i5 or equivalent
- **Memory:** 4 GB RAM
- **Graphics:** DirectX 11 compatible GPU
- **Webcam:** Required for gesture recognition
- **Storage:** 500 MB available space

### Recommended Requirements
- **OS:** Windows 10/11
- **Processor:** Intel Core i7 or equivalent
- **Memory:** 8 GB RAM
- **Graphics:** Dedicated GPU with 2GB VRAM
- **Webcam:** HD webcam with good lighting
- **Storage:** 1 GB available space

## Installation Instructions

1. **Download** the game archive from the link above
2. **Extract** the ZIP file to your desired location
3. **Run** `WizardsGame.exe`
4. **Position yourself** in front of your webcam
5. **Start playing!**

## Setup Tips

### Webcam Positioning
- Position your webcam at chest height
- Ensure your hands are fully visible in the frame
- Maintain good lighting - avoid backlighting
- Keep a distance of 2-3 feet from the camera

### Lighting Recommendations
- Use front-facing light sources
- Avoid shadows on your hands
- Natural daylight works best
- If using artificial light, ensure even illumination

## Troubleshooting

### Webcam Not Detected
- Check webcam permissions in Windows settings
- Ensure no other application is using the webcam
- Try restarting the game

### Gesture Recognition Issues
- Improve lighting conditions
- Ensure hands are clearly visible
- Make gestures deliberately, not too fast
- Check that both hands are in frame

### Performance Issues
- Close other applications
- Update graphics drivers
- Lower game resolution in settings
- Ensure your system meets minimum requirements
