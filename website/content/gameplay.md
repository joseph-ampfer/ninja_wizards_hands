# Gameplay Tutorial

Learn how to cast spells with hand gestures and defeat your opponent!

## What is Wizards Game?

A Unity-based 1v1 combat game where you use **hand gestures** to cast spells against an AI opponent.

- Cast spells using your webcam and hand gestures
- Strategic combat like a magical rock-paper-scissors
- No VR headset required!

## Getting Started

1. **Launch the game**
2. **Position yourself** in front of your webcam
3. **Show both hands** to the camera
4. The game will start tracking your gestures

**Tip:** Make sure you have good lighting and your hands are clearly visible!

## Basic Controls

- **ESC** - Pause menu (return to main menu)
- **Spacebar** - Manual cast/confirm spell
- **Webcam** - Gesture recognition (keep hands visible)

## Combat Basics

**Your Goal:** Defeat the AI wizard by reducing their health to zero

**Strategy:**
- Choose offensive spells to damage the enemy
- Use defensive spells like Shield to block attacks
- Dodge incoming projectiles
- Manage your mana wisely
